Our platform allows you to find travel guides, book hotels, and connect with other travelers. We also offer a variety of features that make it easy to explore Nepal, such as location-based notifications and a community forum.

 

I hope you will consider using Trexplore for your next trip to Nepal. We are committed to providing you with a safe, enjoyable, and memorable experience.

I am Bivek Raj Shakya, founder of Trexplore. Nepal is a country that relies heavily on tourism, and I have seen firsthand how tourists can be mistreated or scammed by travel agencies. Trexplore was created to provide a safe and reliable way for tourists to travel Nepal.
